## Translation Contribution Agreement

**Notice regarding translation permission**: The translation work for win12 is maintained collectively by volunteers. By publishing this project on public platforms such as Crowdin and GitHub and inviting you to participate, we propose the following agreement: you agree to make your improvements to win12 translation files available to the win12 project under the [CC0 License](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/legalcode).

In return, you may publicly claim attribution for the translations you have contributed; and if your translations are adopted by the project, you may (but are not required to) submit a patch to add appropriate attribution notices in the repository's `TRANSLATORS` file or other acknowledgments section.

Although this translation contribution agreement does not obligate the project to accept your textual contributions, your participation in the win12 community is welcomed and appreciated.

## How to Contribute

1. Join the team on Crowdin to get started.
2. You may also join our [community chat](https://nerimity.com/i/nyBOr) to discuss translations.
